Rules
  • Maze Runners is a 2-player puzzle game where players will race each other to escape the maze.
  • Players will take turns completing the maze.
  • Multiple paths to the finish line.
  • Players can choose to pick up items inside the maze which will reduce the overall time of the players run.
  • The player with the fastest time wins.
Controls
Move forward
W / KEY UP
Move left
A / KEY LEFT
Move backward
S / KEY DOWN
Move right
D / KEY RIGHT
